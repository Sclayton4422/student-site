Class rules: be promote, be prepared, be positive, be productive, be polite. Grading system: in this class, your grades are based on your proficiency of each standard in the class. In this course, we will use the three modes of communication from the Arizona native and world languages standards for our standard categories. Each standard is designed for students to understand how language functions within the three domains of introspective, interpersonal, and presentational domains.
